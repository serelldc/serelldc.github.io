About

Most media buyers learn on someone else's ad budget. I learned on mine. I founded and scaled KetoMuna (SRDC) from zero — built the funnels, wrote the copy, set up the pixel, and managed every dollar of ad spend myself. That self-funded brand now has verified paying customers in the USA, South Korea, and Dubai, proof the system works before I ever touch a client's budget.

That's the difference I bring: I don't just "run ads" — I think like the business owner footing the bill, because I've been one. Every campaign is built around one question: is this making money, not just generating clicks.

I'm 100% certified in FMBM Version 4 by Dearly Plinky (94/94 lessons) — covering full Meta Ads strategy, pixel tracking, audience targeting, and campaign scaling — plus the Media Buyer Roadmap 2026 for professional client onboarding, reporting, and delivery.

If you're looking for someone to manage your ad spend like it's their own money — that's exactly what I do.
